FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

An FAQ is a list of frequently asked questions (FAQs) and answers on a particular topic (also known as Questions and Answers [Q&A] or Frequently Asked Questions). Here are some eSport related FAQs.

  • What exactly is eSports?

    Esports is a form of sport competition using video games. Esports often takes the form of organized, multiplayer video game competitions, particularly between professional players, individually or as teams

  • What is the future of eSports in India?

    The present situation of eSports is definitely better than before with the introduction of leagues for neglected eSports. We are on a good path. But still, there is much to be done to produce more world class gamers and to inspire future generations to take eSports as a career. Thereby the fast growing eSports industry will create more job opportunities too.

  • Are eSports players athletes?

    In order to be considered an athlete, you need to be involved in a sport that involves physical strength, speed OR endurance. While strength isn't required, speed and endurance definitely are which is why eSports players can be called athletes.
